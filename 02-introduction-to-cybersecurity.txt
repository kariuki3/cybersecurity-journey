INTRODUCTION TO CYBERSECURITY

🔹 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting:
Computers
Networks
Data
from unauthorized access, attacks, or damage.

🔹 The CIA Triad (VERY IMPORTANT)

These are the 3 core principles of security:

Confidentiality > Only authorized users can access data
Example: Passwords, encryption
Integrity > Data should not be altered or tampered with
Example: Hashing (ensures files are unchanged)
Availability > Systems must be accessible when needed
Example: Preventing DDoS attacks


🔹 Types of Cybersecurity Roles

🔴 Offensive Security (Red Team)
Attack systems to find vulnerabilities
Tools: Kali Linux, Metasploit
Goal: Think like a hacker

🔵 Defensive Security (Blue Team)
Protect and monitor systems
Tools: SIEM, firewalls
Goal: Detect and stop attacks

🟣 Purple Team
Combines Red + Blue teams

🔹 Common Threats
Malware (viruses, ransomware)
Phishing (fake emails)
Brute-force attacks
Social engineering
